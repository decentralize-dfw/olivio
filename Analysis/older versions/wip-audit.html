Phase 2 — Conversion Infrastructure
Complete within 30–60 days post-launch · Estimated effort: 3–4 weeks
2.1 · Abandoned cart email flow (Klaviyo)
HIGHScore: 07
Industry standard: 5–15% of abandoned carts recovered via email. For a €30–€70 AOV, each recovered cart is significant.
  • Email 1 (1 hour after abandonment): "You left something behind" — product image, name, price, direct link to cart
  • Email 2 (24 hours): "Still thinking?" — add size guide link + one customer review quote + "We respond within 24h if you have questions"
  • Email 3 (72 hours): "Last chance" — 5% off code with 48h expiry + social proof (review count)
  • Do not show discount in Email 1 — many will convert without it
KlaviyoShopify Checkout
2.2 · Launch loyalty/rewards programme (Smile.io)
HIGHScore: 07
Natural repeat-purchase dynamic: children grow through 6 age categories over ~12 years.
  • Install Smile.io — free tier supports up to 200 monthly orders
  • Points: 1 point per €1 spent. 100 points = €5 reward.
  • Bonus points: +50 for a photo review, +30 for social share, +100 for referring a friend (friend gets 10% off first order)
  • Birthday reward: double points in the birth month of the child (ask during account setup)
  • "Age upgrade" campaign: when a child's size category changes, email: "Time to size up? Use your rewards points."
